State Legislative News

Welcome to the Montana Department of the American Legion State Legislative News for the 66th Montana Legislative Session which began Monday, January 7th, 2019. Please check this site often as you will see periodic updates of the 66th Session process as they relate to Montana Veterans and Service Members. Below are some useful links for you to use to keep current on specific legislation and contact your legislator.
